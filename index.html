ABOUT OUR HERO

The Story

Australian web television series created for Stan by LiquidLight Productions (LLp), based on the Hargisomo Comic character of the same name. It is set in the Stan Cinematic Universe (SCU), sharing continuity with the films of the franchise and is the next addition in the universe of shows that lead to The Protectors crossover miniseries. The series will also be produced by LLp in association with Stan.

 

Dexter Hero Adams stars as the show-runner, a former convict with superhuman strength and supernatural powers who now fights crime. Development of the series began in late 2016. In December 2016, Adams was cast as the hero, and in 2017 appeared first in Stan's original show Wonder Girl, as an introduction to the character. The series is filmed in Sydney Australia.

 

All episodes of the first season are available to stream NOW. Early viewings were met with positive reviews. In  February 2018, Stan renewed 'Untouchable' for a second season, set to be released in 2019, due to a massive outcry by fans for a new season
